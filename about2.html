Dances That We Teach

  1. Pop Dance

Pop Dance is a popular music subgenre that originated in the late 1970s to early 1980s. Developing from a combination of dance and pop with influences of disco, post-disco and synth-pop, it is generally characterised by strong beats with easy, uncomplicated song structures. The genre, on the whole, tends to be producer-driven, despite some notable exceptions.
